Jo Neville

Paper Artist

Jo Neville is the artist and designer behind Paper Couture, one of Sydney’s most respected creative paper practices. She is the current artist in resident at Q Station, North Head, Manly.


Paper Couture produces bespoke work for retail and corporate activations, event decor and custom art installations.


Paper Couture installations and displays are always innovative, original and impeccably crafted.
